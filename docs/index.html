Part 1: Customer-Facing Checkout Flow

Follow these steps to complete a purchase on the Allergan Aesthetics store

1

Access the Homepage

Navigate to the Allergan Aesthetics BigCommerce store to begin your shopping experience.

  • Open your web browser
  • Go to: https://allergan-aesthetics.mybigcommerce.com/
  • The homepage will display featured products and main navigation
Screenshot: Homepage
Important Note
Ensure you have your account credentials ready. If you don't have an account, you may need to register before completing checkout.
2

Sign In to Your Account

Log in with your existing credentials to access your account and proceed with checkout.

  • Click on the "Sign In" or "Account" link (typically in the top right corner)
  • Enter your registered email address
  • Enter your password
  • Click "Sign In" or "Log In" button
Screenshot: Sign In Page
Account Required
You must be logged in to complete the checkout process. If you're a new customer, contact your Allergan Aesthetics representative for account setup.
3

Browse Products

Explore the product catalog to find the items you need.

  • Use the main navigation menu to browse product categories
  • Use the search function to find specific products
  • Filter products by category, brand, or other attributes
  • View product listings with images, names, and prices
Screenshot: Product Browsing
4

Select a Product

Click on a product to view its detailed information page.

  • Click on the product image or name
  • Review product details, description, and specifications
  • Check product availability
  • Select quantity if applicable
  • Choose any product options or variants
Screenshot: Product Detail Page
5

Add to Cart

Add your selected product to the shopping cart.

  • Click the "Add to Cart" button
  • Wait for confirmation that the item was added
  • You may see a mini cart popup or notification
  • Continue shopping or proceed to cart
Screenshot: Add to Cart Action
Success
You should see a confirmation message that your product has been added to the cart. The cart icon may update to show the number of items.
6

View Shopping Cart & Initiate Checkout

Review all items in your shopping cart and begin the checkout process.

  • Click on the cart icon or "View Cart" link
  • Review all items in your cart
  • Verify quantities are correct
  • Update quantities if needed
  • Remove items if necessary
  • Review subtotal
  • Click the "Checkout" or "Proceed to Checkout" button
Screenshot: Shopping Cart & Checkout
Cart Review
This is your last chance to modify your order before checkout. Double-check all items and quantities.
7

Enter Shipping Information

Provide the delivery address and shipping details for your order.

  • Enter or verify your shipping address
  • Fill in required fields: First Name, Last Name
  • Enter Company Name (if applicable)
  • Enter Street Address
  • Enter City, State/Province, and ZIP/Postal Code
  • Select Country
  • Enter Phone Number
Screenshot: Shipping Information Form
Address Accuracy
Ensure all shipping information is accurate to avoid delivery delays or issues. This address will be used for product shipment.
8

Complete Custom Fields (CRITICAL)

This is the most important step - complete the three mandatory custom fields that are unique to Allergan Aesthetics checkout.

Required Custom Fields

These three fields MUST be completed during checkout. They appear in the shipping information section.

1. PREFERRED SHIPPING METHOD
Specify how you want your order shipped (e.g., Ground, Express, Overnight, Standard)
2. ARRIVAL DATE
Enter the date when you need the products to arrive at your location
3. PO NUMBER
Enter your Purchase Order Number for internal tracking and billing purposes
Screenshot: Custom Fields in Checkout
Custom Fields Display
Replace with actual screenshot clearly showing the three custom fields: PREFERRED SHIPPING METHOD, ARRIVAL DATE, and PO NUMBER in the checkout form
Critical Information
These fields are mandatory and unique to this store. Make sure to:
  • Fill in all three custom fields before proceeding
  • Use accurate information as this affects order processing
  • The PO NUMBER is essential for billing reconciliation
  • The ARRIVAL DATE helps with scheduling and logistics
Shipping Costs Note
You may see a message stating "SHIPPING COSTS SENT SEPARATELY". This means shipping charges will be calculated and invoiced separately from your product order. Do not expect to see shipping costs in your current order total.
9

Complete Billing, Payment & Review Order

Provide billing information, select payment method, and review your complete order.

  • Check "Same as shipping address" if applicable, or enter different billing address
  • Select from available payment methods (Credit Card, PayPal, etc.)
  • Enter payment details securely
  • Review all items in your order
  • Verify quantities, pricing, shipping and billing addresses
  • Confirm the three custom fields are filled: PREFERRED SHIPPING METHOD, ARRIVAL DATE, PO NUMBER
  • Review order total
  • Read and accept terms and conditions if required
Final Check
This is your last opportunity to review everything. Make sure:
  • All products and quantities are correct
  • Shipping address is accurate
  • Custom fields (PREFERRED SHIPPING METHOD, ARRIVAL DATE, PO NUMBER) are completed
  • Payment information is correct
10

Place Order & Confirmation

Complete the order and receive confirmation.

  • Click "Place Order" or "Complete Order" button
  • Wait for order processing (do not refresh the page)
  • You will be redirected to a confirmation page
  • Note your order number
  • You should receive a confirmation email
Screenshot: Order Confirmation
Order Placed Successfully
Congratulations! Your order has been placed. Save your order number for reference. You will receive a confirmation email with order details. Remember that shipping costs will be sent separately as indicated during checkout.

Part 2: Admin Backend Order Management

Access and manage orders through the BigCommerce admin panel

1

Access Admin & Navigate to Orders

Log in to the BigCommerce admin panel and navigate to the orders section.

  • Go to: https://store-s9ui47mdcv.mybigcommerce.com/manage
  • Enter your admin credentials and log in
  • After logging in, look for the left sidebar navigation menu
  • Click on "Orders" in the main navigation
  • You'll see a list of all orders in the system
  • Use filters/search to find specific orders
  • Click on an order to view its details
Screenshot: Admin Dashboard - Orders Navigation
Admin Access Required
You must have administrator credentials to access the backend. Contact your store owner or IT administrator if you don't have access.
2

View Order Details & Custom Fields

Access detailed order information including the three critical custom fields.

  • Click on an order number or "View" button to open order details
  • The order detail page displays comprehensive order information
  • Review customer information
  • Review products ordered
  • Review shipping and billing addresses
  • Scroll to find the custom fields section
Screenshot: Order Detail View with Custom Fields
Custom Fields Location in Admin

The three custom fields from checkout are displayed in the order details page. Look for these fields:

PREFERRED SHIPPING METHOD
Shows the customer's preferred shipping option
ARRIVAL DATE
Shows when the customer needs the products to arrive
PO NUMBER
Shows the customer's Purchase Order Number for billing
Critical for Order Processing
These custom fields are essential for fulfillment:
  • PREFERRED SHIPPING METHOD - Use this to select the correct shipping carrier and service
  • ARRIVAL DATE - Ensure shipment is scheduled to meet this deadline
  • PO NUMBER - Include this on all invoices and shipping documents
Order Processing Workflow
When processing orders:
  1. Review all custom fields immediately
  2. Use the PREFERRED SHIPPING METHOD to book shipment
  3. Schedule shipment to meet the ARRIVAL DATE
  4. Reference the PO NUMBER on all documentation
  5. Calculate and invoice shipping costs separately as noted
  6. Update order status as you process
